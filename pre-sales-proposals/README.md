# Pre-Sales & Technical Proposals
Templates and methodologies for estimating complex migrations and GenAI implementations.

## Methodologies
* **Value Engineering:** Focus on optimizing the function-to-cost ratio and demonstrating tangible ROI.
* **Consultative Selling:** Diagnosing client pain points to tailor bespoke technical solutions.
* **TCO Analysis:** Total Cost of Ownership modeling (CapEx vs. OpEx) for cloud migrations.

## Artifacts
* RFP Responses, Sizing Spreadsheets, and Proof of Concept (PoC) success criteria.
